About Us

Utah's Brightest Choice Since 2021

Luminary Lights Co. was founded in 2021 with a simple vision: bring professional-grade holiday and architectural lighting to the Wasatch Front. What started as a holiday display company has grown into Utah's most in-demand lighting installer.

Every display we install uses premium LED technology — delivering richer color, longer life, and lower energy costs than traditional lighting. We design, install, remove, and store everything, so you never have to touch a single strand.

As part of the Topaz Holdings Group, we operate with full insurance, trained crews, and enterprise-level service standards on every job — no matter the size.
